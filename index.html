Introduction of Resort

Welcome To Roop Vihar Resorts

Since its inception in the year 2009 Roop Vihar Hotels & Resorts has built a strong reputation for offering luxurious rooms and sprawling lush green lawns making it a grand venue for lodging, get-togethers, wedding events and conferences. Located away from the pandemonium of the city, it offers a peaceful stay and is yet close enough to the travel and business destinations of the Silk city. It is located on Suraj Mohan Thakur (Barari) Road just 5.4 kms away from Bhagalpur Railway Station and is easily accessible.
Roop Vihar boasts of indulging its guests with a swimming pool, arched huts, organic gardens and serene landscapes that encourage not just fun and frolic but solitude and meditation too.
The establishment has been chosen close to the bank of the holy Ganga to blend perfectly with its surroundings and is uniquely uplifting from the ordinary.
